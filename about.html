About FAWAD Lab

The FAWAD Lab at the University of Canberra is dedicated to research in intelligent systems for rehabilitation robotics, neuro-mechanical control, and AI-powered medical devices. We explore the frontiers of machine learning, reinforcement learning, and human-robot interaction.

Research Capabilities

  • Musculoskeletal Modeling and Simulation
  • Reinforcement Learning and Control
  • EMG Signal Processing
  • Human-Robot Interaction
  • Transformer Models for Motion Prediction
  • Biomechanics and Neuroplasticity
  • Sensor Fusion and Wearable Technologies

Projects and Grants

  • UC Smart Shoulder Rehabilitation System, UC Research Grant, 2025–2027
  • Quantum-Enhanced Trajectory Optimization, ARC Discovery, 2024–2026
  • AI for Neuromechanical Adaptation in Exoskeletons, 2023–2025
  • Reinforcement Learning in Neuro-Rehabilitation, NHMRC Pilot Grant, 2025
